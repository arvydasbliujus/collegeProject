Full-Time Course Fees 2015 / 2016

Fees shown below for all HND courses include registration, tuition and assessment fee. Unless otherwise stated, books and other study materials are not included. Alternatively, funds may be remitted by direct transfer to the following bank account: Inter-Ed UK Ltd., Barclays Bank, 99 Hatton Garden, London, EC1N 8DN, sorting code 20-37-75, account number 60934356. IBAN GB55 BARC 2037 7560 9343 56.
