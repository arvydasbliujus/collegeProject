How to get to The City College

The following buses pass through Old Street roundabout: 43, X43, 55, 141, 214, 243, 271, 309 and 505

The nearest underground station is Old Street the signs to Subway 1 - City Road North (East Side) Regents Canal and follow the steps up to East Road, which leads into City Road. Follow City Road for about 100 yards and at the traffic lights bear to your right, where East Road begins. Keep walking for another 200 yards and you will find The City College.
