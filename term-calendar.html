HND Term Dates 2015 / 2016

The following is the calendar for the academic year 2015/2016. In addition to the holiday periods mentioned below, there will be no classes on 4 May, 25 May 2015 & 02 May, 30 May 2016 which are UK Bank Holidays.

All assessment dates are provisional and may be subject to change – any changes will be advised on the Notice Board in Reception.
